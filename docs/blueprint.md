# **App Name**: HSC Success Planner

## Core Features:

- Interactive Dashboard: A personalized home page providing an overview of upcoming revisions and current goals, offering quick action buttons.
- Syllabus Management: A comprehensive view to see a chapter list and topics, and to update topic details.
- Goal Setting & Tracking: Enable the setting and tracking of study goals - with insights from recent weeks
- Study Session Analytics: Generate the insights from recorded study sessions.
- Adaptive Routine Generation: Automatically generate an optimal routine based on the exam, with study time distributed, balanced based on syllabus data and the user's daily avilability.
- Personalized Revision Plan: Algorithm that provides the revision queue by the status from subjects page.
- Data Persistence: Store the application and student data to window.storage api.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A calm blue (#5DADE2) to evoke a sense of focus and tranquility, reducing anxiety associated with exam preparation.
- Background color: Light, desaturated blue (#EBF5FB) to maintain a calm and clean interface.
- Accent color: A soft teal (#45B39D) to draw attention to important actions and notifications, promoting a positive and proactive study environment.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif with a modern, neutral feel, that's suitable for both headlines and body text
- Consistent use of Lucide React icons to provide clear visual cues. Ensure all icons are uniformly styled, using thin lines and a simple design to maintain a clean and modern aesthetic.
- A card-based design to organize the large amounts of information in a consistent layout, ensuring students see all their tasks, lessons, routines at a single glance.
- Subtle transition effects when revealing new content to keep users from becoming overwhelmed, ensuring interactions remain smooth and seamless.
